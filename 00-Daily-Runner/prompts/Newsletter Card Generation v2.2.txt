# Newsletter Card Generation v2.2
**VERSION:** 2.2  
**DATE:** 2025-10-08  
**CHANGE:** Score threshold lowered to 50, top 8-12 articles

---

## STEP 1: Query Top Articles
```sql
SELECT id, title, url, pmo_score, full_content 
FROM daily_insights 
WHERE published_date = DATE('now') 
  AND content_fetched = 1
  AND pmo_score >= 50
ORDER BY pmo_score DESC 
LIMIT 12
```

---

## STEP 2: Generate Cards

For each article:

**Tagline:** 12 words max, benefit-focused hook

**TLDR:** 4 bullets, 20-30 words each
- PMO pain point solved
- AI features/capabilities  
- Quick action steps
- Specific use cases

**Badges** (exact values only):
- **pmo_area:** Risk|Scope|Schedule|Cost|Quality|Resource|Stakeholder|Integration|Procurement
- **implementation_speed:** Quick Win|Weeks to Deploy|Long to Deploy
- **skill_level:** Beginner|Intermediate|Advanced|Expert
- **price_info:** Free|Freemium|Paid|Enterprise
- **read_time:** word_count/200 rounded
